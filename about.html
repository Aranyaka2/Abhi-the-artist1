About ABHI

A Visionary Artist Bridging Tradition and Innovation

Born in Chalissery, Palakkad district, Abhi is an artist with a natural flair for art and an unquenchable passion to dream beyond boundaries. Recognizing art as the canvas for his life’s journey, he embarked on a path that would define his legacy.

Sustainable Artistry: The Use of Natural Colors

Abhi is deeply committed to preserving the authenticity and sustainability of traditional art forms. He meticulously extracts pigments from natural sources such as stones, leaves, and minerals, ensuring that each piece of art is not only visually stunning but also environmentally responsible. This practice reflects his reverence for nature and his desire to honor the rich artistic heritage of Kerala, where natural dyes have been used for centuries in mural paintings and other art forms.

By using these organic materials, Abhi creates works that are timeless, vibrant, and imbued with the essence of the earth. His dedication to this craft underscores his belief that art should harmonize with the environment rather than exploit it.

Global Aspirations: Bridging Cultures Through Art

He seeks to share his unique blend of traditional and contemporary art with a global audience. His work has already garnered acclaim in Dubai, where he continues to push creative boundaries by blending mural art with abstract aesthetics.

With aspirations to collaborate with artists, institutions, and communities worldwide, Abhi envisions his art as a bridge between cultures. His ability to seamlessly fuse Kerala’s artistic traditions with modern techniques positions him as a true global ambassador for sustainable and culturally resonant art.

Artistic Journey

Abhi’s formal training began with a 5-year National Diploma in Mural Painting from the Guruvayur Institute, where he graduated with first-class honours. Over 20 years, he has mastered diverse styles including mural painting, modern art, texture art, abstract art, graffiti, minimalist art, 3D art, and cement relief wall art.

Notable Projects

  • 2,000 sq. ft. Mural at Koodalmanikyam Temple
    Celebrating Kerala’s spiritual heritage through vibrant depictions of rituals and deities.
  • Cultural Murals at PK Das Hospital
    Showcasing Kerala’s art, folklore, and healthcare traditions in a modern healthcare setting.
  • Cement Relief Wall
    A 3D masterpiece featuring global cultural scripts and art forms, earning national acclaim.
  • fusion-art
    Fusion Art at Thrissur DYSP Office Kerala.

Current Work

Based in Dubai, Abhi continues to push creative boundaries, blending mural art with abstract aesthetics. His freelance practice spans large-scale public installations, private commissions, and collaborations in the film industry.
