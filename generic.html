Mood Boards

A curated exploration of artistic inspiration, blending tradition, innovation, and emotion.

These mood boards serve as visual blueprints for my creative process, where Kerala's rich cultural heritage meets contemporary minimalism. Each board is a thoughtful synthesis of textures, colors, and motifs that narrate a story—bridging the past and the future. From intricate temple carvings to bold geometric forms, these collages encapsulate the essence of inspiration and guide the transformation of ideas into large-scale murals that evoke emotion and cultural resonance.

The juxtaposition of elements reflects my signature style: abstract expressionism intertwined with cultural storytelling. Earthy tones like ochre and terracotta anchor the compositions, paying homage to Kerala’s landscapes, while modern accents such as neon highlights and metallic textures introduce a contemporary edge. The interplay of rough graffiti tags with smooth 3D cement reliefs creates a dynamic tension, embodying the harmony of chaos and order. These mood boards are not merely sketches but strategic frameworks for crafting art that is both timeless and boldly original.
